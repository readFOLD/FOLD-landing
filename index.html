FOLD is a context creation platform for journalists and storytellers, allowing them to structure and craft complex stories.
FOLD helps readers confidently engage with new material, providing curated tangents to capture interest and attention.

FOLD is built by Alexis Hope, Kevin Hu, and Joe Goldbeck at the MIT Media Lab and will launch for open beta on April 7th, 2015.
